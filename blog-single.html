The world of crypto is full of potential, but it also comes with considerable risks. Market volatility is high, and the drastic fluctuations in asset prices constantly expose investors to risk. For those seeking steady returns, choices are limited. But now, the AlphaForce protocol is changing the scene.

What is AlphaForce?

AlphaForce is a Web3 product that allows you to deposit USDC and then earn more than a 25% return, regardless of market fluctuations. This return is continuous, stable, and backed by solid historical performance. AlphaForce aims to provide a safe and efficient solution for investors seeking steady returns in the crypto world.

Features

AlphaForce employs Zero-Knowledge Proofs and Proof of Reserve to ensure the security of user assets. It offers a stable annualized return of over 25%, and future versions can add leverage to achieve returns of over 80%. AlphaForce's "Novel 1" product has minimal maximum drawdown, with the largest drawdown of only 1.1% in its five-year performance record.

AlphaForce operates a all-weather strategy, unaffected by market volatility. The strategy team has years of trading experience and has managed billions of dollars in crypto assets. AlphaForce introduces a novel source of yield, bringing competitive returns into the DeFi space. It connects DeFi capital with various innovative off-chain yield types, offering attractive returns while remaining transparent and permissionless.

Usage Scenarios

AlphaForce can be used in any scenario as it provides stable earnings regardless of market fluctuations - a valuable feature in the crypto world.

Community-driven

In the context of Web3, AlphaForce will be a community-driven protocol. AlphaForce Token (AFT) will be fairly distributed to users participating in the protocol, allowing everyone to share in the growth of AlphaForce and earn additional returns.

Vision

The vision of AlphaForce is to introduce professional quantitative strategies and AI strategies into the current crypto world. We use AI to optimize and iterate strategies, bringing a new source of returns to crypto users.
